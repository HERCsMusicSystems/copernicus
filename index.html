Lodge Copernicus is a Freemasons Lodge.

Contrary to other Craft lodges, Copernicus works in FIVE degrees (rather than typical three degrees). As far as we know, no other lodge in the world works in five degrees at the moment. The five-degree structure provides us with time and cost effective way of practicing freemasonry in multiple degrees.

Every lodge is unique in its own way. At copernicus we put a strong emphasis becoming better men through masonic education and perfecting our rituals. If you are serious about becoming a better man and a serious freemasons, then perhaps Lodge Copernicus might be a good lodge for you.
