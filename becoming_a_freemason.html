Becoming a Freemason

First thing to do is to make contact!

Freemasons do not seek out or approach men to become candidates, preferring those who are interested in becoming better men1 to seek us to start their journey in Freemasonry.

Simply introduce yourself to us by writing to our Secretary at: secretary@copernicus.org.au

Once we receive your email we will contact you and arrange to meet with you to answer any questions you might have and explain how it all works.


1 - Lodge Copernicus is a men only Lodge but there are also women only and mixed gender Lodges based in South Australia.
