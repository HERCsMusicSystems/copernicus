Degrees Worked

Typically, most Lodges work the three degrees of Entered Apprentice, Fellow Craft and Master Mason. Lodge Copernicus is a unique lodge in that it works the five degrees of pure and antient Freemasonry - the Craft, the Mark and the Royal Arch Chapter - all under a single Warrant.

The full suite of degrees that we work is as follows:

Craft Lodge
  • 1st Entered Apprentice
  • 2nd Fellowcraft
  • 3rd Master Mason
Mark Lodge
  • Mark Master Mason
  • Royal Ark Mariner (adjunct degree)
Royal Arch Chapter
  • Excellent Master Mason
  • Royal Arch
  • Red Cross of Babylon (adjunct degree)
  • Excellent Chief (adjunct degree)
